City Latitude Versus Cloudiness

Here we see cloudiness does not appear to be strongly correlated with the latitude of the city. There is an even distribution for both northern and southern hemisphers. Some grouping is visible at the cloudiness bounds of 0 and 100, but this is visible for all latitudes as we might expect.
